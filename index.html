About

Hello! I am Edward, a Software Engineer currently based in New York City.

Throughout my career, I have enjoyed many experiences working in the Information Technology industry. As a former IT Instructor at Per Scholas, I helped prepare students from underrepresented communities for their first technical job. As a Technician at Google, I was fortunate to work with some of the most talented Technicians and Engineers the world has to offer. However, as I continued to progress in my career and learn new technologies, I decided to shift my area of focus to Full-Stack Web Development and Software Engineering.

As a Software Engineer, I enjoy applying my skills and knowledge of Full-Stack Web Development and Computer Science to build attractive and dynamic user interfaces that enhance productivity and provide a better online presence for companies.

When I am not working on a project, I use my spare time to learn more about popular libraries, frameworks, and other programming languages.

I am always excited to hear from former colleagues, managers or interesting creative people, so feel free to contact me if you would like to connect.
